About Me

Hello! I'm a dedicated freshman student at the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania, pursuing dual concentrations in Accounting and Finance. With hands-on experience in negotiation research, tax preparation, and business development, I'm passionate about helping individuals and businesses make informed financial decisions.

My background began with volunteering at the WellSpan York Cancer Center, creating origami flowers for cancer patients undergoing chemotherapy. The flowers gave hope to hundreds, "Just like these flowers, I too won't wither away." My volunteer experiences stretched further into my community, working for multiple underserved families with tax preparation through the IRS's Volunteer Income Assistance Program. On a professional level, I've interned with Flickingers & Co. and ProliFix Accounting, gaining invaluable experience in the financial and accounting field. I'm also deeply committed to entrepreneurship and community development through various leadership roles.
